To evaluate a job description, copy it and paste it into your Claude conversation starting with /jd-review. Claude will analyse it against your profile and automatically add it to this tracker.

3. Claude analyses it against your profile (salary expectations, work type preferences, domain fit, screening likelihood)
4. The role is automatically added here with the verdict and assessment notes
